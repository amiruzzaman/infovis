The task for this homework is to develop five different visualization using five different dataset.
Following data sets are given:

  • Car: this dataset consists of three different categories, such as, American, European, and Japanese made cars. In addition, each categories have the different types of cars, based on their mileage per gallon, horsepower, weights, number of cylinders, acceleration, etc.

  • Cereal: this dataset consists of different brand of cereals, categorized by manufacturer, and type. In addition, each brand is associated with calories, fat, sodium, etc.

  • Film: the film dataset is grouped by different subject areas, such as, drama, action, comedy, etc. In addition, each film has its own length, and year associated with it.

  • Grocery: this dataset have divided grocery expenses based on gender (e.g., male, female). The dataset includes age, income, and payment type of each person.

  • Fund: the fund or mutual fund dataset is categorized by different types of mutual fund agencies, such as, international, large growth, large value, small growth, and small value, etc. Each type also has their net assets information, year to date (YTD), 5-year, and 10-year income information.
